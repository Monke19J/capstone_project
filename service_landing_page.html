Our Services

Unified Inventory Tracking

Comprehensive tracking system for both reagents and equipment in a single platform. Monitor stock levels, equipment status, and location information in real-time with detailed item-specific attributes and history.

Smart Expiration Alerts

Proactive notification system that alerts users one month before reagent expiration dates. Customizable alert thresholds with escalating reminders ensure critical reagents are never used past their expiration date.

Preventive Maintenance

Automated scheduling and tracking of equipment maintenance activities based on usage patterns and manufacturer recommendations.

Analytics

Comprehensive reporting dashboard measuring total quantities of reagents and equipment, with detailed monthly analysis of inventory movement.

Schedule Management

Intelligent schedule tracking system for equipment reservations, maintenance windows, and calibration events. Prevent scheduling conflicts and maximize equipment uptime through optimized planning.
